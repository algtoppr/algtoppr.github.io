Robert Ghirst

Harmonic Balance: Using Geometry & Topology to Agree

What does it mean to "agree to disagree"? Is that possible? What happens when people communicate over social media and have different viewpoints? This talk will approach issues of balancing forces in tension using the beauty of topology and geometry. There is harmonic convergence in all types of information systems, whether physical, economic, or even social. This talk will converge to some deep mathematical ideas, but in a fun and gentle manner that requires very little background in math besides an active imagination and a bit of matrix algebra.
