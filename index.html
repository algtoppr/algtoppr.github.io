About

This is the website for an algebraic topology conference series meeting every two years in San Juan, Puerto Rico, organized by Mona Merling, Jeremy Miller, Manuel Rivera, and Natalia Pacheco Tallaj. The conferences will feature a variety of events focusing on recent developments in algebraic topology and their applications to geometry, physics, and data science.

The second iteration of the conference will occur on June 5 - June 14, 2024. It will include mini-courses, invited research talks, contributed talks, as well as a general public event.

Registration information for the 2024 conference will be available on this site soon.
