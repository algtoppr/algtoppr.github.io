About

This is the website for an algebraic topology conference series meeting every two years in San Juan, Puerto Rico. Each conference features a variety of events (mini-courses and lecture series, research talks, contributed talks, and general public talks) focusing on recent developments in algebraic topology and their applications to other fields of mathematics, physics, and data science. The next iteration of the series will occur on July 10 - 17, 2026 right before the ICM 2026. Save the date and stay tuned for more information. Click here for more information on past conferences.
